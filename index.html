About Me


I am a proud mother of 2 who is a hard working creative thinker. I have a bachelor's degree in Commerce and accounting. I am passionate about arts and I teach kids Indian classical dance. Lastly, I am driven by my desire to make a difference in people’s lives. In terms of where I am in my professional life, I haven't worked full time in over 10 years due to various different reasons. Initially it was because of my visa restrictions and later it was because I wanted to stay home with the kids. Now, I want to have a career instead of just a job and I was looking for a structured course to help me get into the IT industry. Here we are. :)
